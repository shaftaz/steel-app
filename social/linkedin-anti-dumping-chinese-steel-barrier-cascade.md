# LinkedIn draft — anti-dumping-chinese-steel-barrier-cascade (do not publish on site)

62 countries. 207 restrictions. A record 395 cumulative trade measures. And Chinese steel exports still doubled since 2020.

The wall isn't failing because it's too low. It's failing because steel reroutes: blocked finished products become surging semis (slab exports +182% in Q1), protected markets push volume to open ones, and enforcement chases origins as far as Algeria (72.94% CVD on rebar).

We call it the barrier cascade — and for buyers, the duty in the headline is rarely what moves your price. The rerouting it triggers is.
